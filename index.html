National Service Training Program
The National Service Training Program (NSTP) is a civic education and defense preparedness program for students in the Philippines, established by the government on November 13, 2009, under Republic Act No. 9163. Its primary aim is to enhance civic consciousness and defense preparedness among the youth, promoting service ethics and patriotism through various training components. NSTP originated from the National Defense Act of 1935 and has evolved to foster nationalism and service among Filipino youth.
